Solo Art Exhibition · Club Unión
Club Unión · San José, Costa Rica · Est. 1923
Exhibited original artwork for an entire week at Club Unión, Costa Rica's most prestigious and historic private club, founded in 1923 and historically the preferred gathering place of the country's political leaders, top business figures, and cultural elite. With its neoclassical architecture, marble floors, and century-old legacy, Club Unión represents the highest tier of cultural distinction in Costa Rica. Having artwork exhibited within its walls is one of the most significant honors an artist can receive in the country.

Artist Statement

"Art has always been my first language. Design came after, but painting never left."

I picked up a paintbrush before I picked up a pencil for school. Growing up in San José, Costa Rica, I trained under professional painting instructors and competed internationally, earning Gold and Silver medals in Japan and top honors in Costa Rica's national competitions.

My art lives at the intersection of the organic and the electric. I'm drawn to color as emotion, to line as language, and to the tension between chaos and control. Whether working in acrylic on canvas or building worlds digitally, my work is always personal.

, Carolina Cravens
