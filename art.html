Art Exhibition · Club Unión de Costa Rica
Club Unión · San José, Costa Rica · Est. 1874
Selected to exhibit original works at the Club Unión de Costa Rica — one of the most exclusive and historic private clubs in Central America, founded in 1874. Having your art displayed within its walls is considered a rare distinction reserved for artists of serious merit. Club Unión has hosted Costa Rica's most prominent figures in culture, politics, and society for over 150 years. To exhibit there is to be recognized as part of Costa Rica's cultural legacy.

Artist Statement

"Art has always been my first language. Design came after — but painting never left."

I picked up a paintbrush before I picked up a pencil for school. Growing up in San José, Costa Rica, I trained under professional painting instructors and competed internationally — earning Gold and Silver medals in Japan and top honors in Costa Rica's national competitions.

My art lives at the intersection of the organic and the electric. I'm drawn to color as emotion, to line as language, and to the tension between chaos and control. Whether working in acrylic on canvas or building worlds digitally, my work is always personal — a record of what it feels like to be fully alive.

— Carolina Cravens
